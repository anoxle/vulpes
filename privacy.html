Vulpes - Privacy Policy

This policy outlines how Vulpes collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and maintaining transparency in our data practices.

1. Information We Collect and Use

To provide and improve our services, we collect the following information:

  • User IDs: Unique identifiers for each user
  • Server IDs: Identifiers for Discord servers where Vulpes is used
  • Usernames: Your Discord username

This information is essential for identifying users, managing databases, and providing personalized services.

2. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We prioritize your privacy and do not share or disclose any personal information collected through Vulpes, except as outlined in this policy or with your explicit consent. However, we may share aggregate and anonymized data for analytical purposes or to improve our services.

3. Updates to the Privacy Policy

anoxle.git and Vulpes Management Team reserve the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated via notices posted on the Vulpes Support Server.

4. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through the following method:
